boymoder

please be kind to boymoders in the wild

a boymoder is a trans woman that for various reasons, social, personal, or otherwise, has decided to secretly medically transition with hrt while still presenting outwardly masculine. often times this facade becomes apparent to others around them as hrt works its magic, while the boymoder obliviously thinks that they don't pass. for more information: Boymoder Awareness Network

ingredients

  • one closeted trans woman
  • one oversized black hooded sweatshirt
  • crippling social anxiety
  • one pair of skinny jeans
  • hrt

steps

  1. be trans woman
  2. take hrt
  3. put on black hoodie
  4. wear skinny jeans
  5. proceed as if cis
  6. optional
    • pluck eyebrows
    • wear sports bra
    • pretend to be tough
